💡 Tips for Creating Exams

  • Ensure your Excel file has clear column headers: Question and Maximum Marks
  • Upload a PDF book or reference document — AI will automatically extract answers
  • Use text-based PDFs for best results (scanned image PDFs are not supported)
  • Review AI-extracted answers before finalizing — you can modify them if needed
  • Set realistic time limits for your exam duration
  • Consider setting passing percentage between 40-60% for fair assessment
  • Exam dates cannot be set to past dates for fairness
  • End time must always be after start time
